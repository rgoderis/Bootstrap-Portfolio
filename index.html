About Me

Ryan Goderis
Currently attending UCF Coding Bootcamp

I am a native Floridian originally from the Orlando area and graduated from the University of Central Florida in 2010 (Go Knights!). As any proud Central Florida resident I love spending time in/on the water, going to theme parks (yes I have annual passes to both Disney and Universal), and know how to outrun an alligator. When I have free time, I'm usually gaming(console, pc, and board), reading especially sci-fi and fantisy, surfing or paddleboarding, or spending time with my adorable nephews.

I hope to become a full stack web developer and am currently enrolled in a web dev bootcamp. I first started writing any code back in October of 2017 and have slowly gotten my feet wet with different languages. I have worked through one full stack course on Udemy but knew that I needed to take the next step and enroll in a boot camp in order to fully immerse myself and take the next step on my path to becoming a developer. My goals are to showcase my skills, contribute to the amazing developer community, and have fun along the way!
