About Me

Ryan Goderis
Currently attending UCF Coding Bootcamp

I am a native Floridian originally from the Orlando area and graduated from the University of Central Florida in 2010 (Go Knights!). As any proud Central Florida resident I love spending time in/on the water, going to theme parks (yes I have annual passes to both Disney and Universal), and know how to outrun an alligator. When I have free time, I'm usually gaming(console, pc, and board), reading especially sci-fi and fantasy, surfing or paddle-boarding, or spending time with my adorable nephews.

I am a Front-End web developer that excels in both traditional and unique work environments with demonstrated ability to learn and adapt to new skills across differing professions. Recently completed a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at the University of Central Florida that focused on MERN. Skilled with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as well as frameworks such a Bootstrap and jQuery. A quick learner that is enthusiastic in tackling new challenges with a passion for learning and developing as a programer. Excited to apply these skills in the future to contribute to a quality and passionate team and have a lasting positive impact with each application involved with.
